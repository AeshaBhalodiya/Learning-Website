Recipe of : Tea

What need :
  • 1 Cup Water
  • 1 spoon Tea Powder
  • 1 spoon suger


How to make :

make stove on , make a steal jar/bowl on stove , put 1 cup of water inside bowl, put 1 spoon tea powder & 1 spoon suger powder, 5 min dhima gas ae ukado , doodh nakho, ufan aave tya sudhi ukado , gadi lo ane pi lo.
